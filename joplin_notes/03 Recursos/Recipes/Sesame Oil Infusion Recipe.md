Making Sesame Oil:
While making sesame oil at home can be challenging due to the need for sesame seeds and proper equipment, you can make a simple sesame oil infusion:

Ingredients:

White sesame seeds: 100g
Neutral oil (like vegetable or canola oil): 250ml
Instructions:

Toast the sesame seeds in a dry skillet over medium heat until they turn golden brown and aromatic. Be careful not to burn them.
Allow the toasted seeds to cool slightly, then grind them into a coarse powder using a blender or mortar and pestle.
Heat the neutral oil in a saucepan over medium heat until warm, but not smoking.
Add the ground sesame seeds to the oil and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring continuously.
Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool completely.
Strain the oil through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a clean bottle or jar.
Store the sesame oil in a cool, dark place.
This homemade sesame oil can add a delicious nutty flavor to your dishes!
