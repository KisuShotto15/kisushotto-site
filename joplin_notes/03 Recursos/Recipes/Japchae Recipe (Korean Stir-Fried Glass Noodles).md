#### Ingredients (for 2 servings):

##### Main Ingredients:
- Sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon): 120g (raw)
- Pork (thinly sliced): 200g (raw)
- Spinach: 100g
- Carrot: 80g
- Bell pepper (any color): 80g
- Onion: 100g
- Shiitake mushrooms: 60g
- Green onions: 30g
- Sesame oil: 1 tablespoon ar%e(15ml)
- Vegetable oil: 1 tablespoon (15ml)
- Sesame seeds: 1 tablespoon (8g)

##### Sauce:
- Soy sauce: 4 tablespoons (60ml)
- Sugar: 2 tablespoons (30g)
- Garlic: 2 cloves, minced (10g)
- Black pepper: 1/2 teaspoon (1g)

### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Noodles:**
   - Boil the sweet potato noodles according to package instructions, usually about 6-8 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water. Cut the noodles with scissors into more manageable lengths.

2. **Prepare the Sauce:**
   - In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, sugar, minced garlic, and black pepper until the sugar is dissolved.

3. **Cook the Pork:**
   - In a large pan or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
   - Add the thinly sliced pork and cook until no longer pink, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.

4. **Cook the Vegetables:**
   - In the same pan, add 1 tablespoon of sesame oil. Add the onions and cook for 2-3 minutes until translucent.
   - Add the carrots, bell pepper, and shiitake mushrooms. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until tender.
   - Add the spinach and green onions, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until wilted.

5. **Combine Everything:**
   - Return the pork to the pan with the vegetables.
   - Add the cooked noodles and pour the sauce over everything.
   - Toss everything together, cooking for another 2-3 minutes until well combined and heated through.
   - Drizzle with the remaining 1 tablespoon of sesame oil and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
